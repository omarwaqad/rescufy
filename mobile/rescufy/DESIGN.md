# Rescufy Design System

## Brand Personality

* Trustworthy
* Professional
* Emergency-focused
* Fast
* Accessible
* Human-centered

The design should feel reliable and calm during stressful situations.

---

## Design Principles

### Emergency First

Critical actions must always be immediately visible.

### Fast Scanning

Users should identify the most important information within 2 seconds.

### High Contrast

Emergency information must remain visible outdoors and in poor conditions.

### Mobile Usability

Large touch targets and one-handed operation.

### Consistency

Reuse patterns, spacing, and components across all screens.

### Minimal Cognitive Load

Remove unnecessary visual noise.

# Dashboard Design Rules

## Driver Dashboard

Always prioritize:

1. Active Emergency
2. Emergency Status
3. Navigation Action
4. Patient Information
5. Hospital Information
6. Availability State

Avoid:

* Dense tables
* Excessive charts
* Unnecessary decorations
* Hidden primary actions

---

# Accessibility

* Large readable typography
* High contrast
* Outdoor visibility
* Arabic RTL support
* English LTR support
* Large touch targets
* Clear iconography

---

# Desired Visual Style

* Modern healthcare application
* Apple Health quality
* Uber Driver clarity
* Google Maps information hierarchy
* Minimalist cards
* Clean spacing
* Strong typography
* Purposeful use of red

The interface should feel trustworthy rather than flashy.
